# Memecoin Trader Skill

This skill provides the necessary procedural knowledge and frameworks to navigate the high-risk, high-reward world of memecoin trading. It covers identifying early opportunities, vetting contracts for safety, and executing trades using specialized tools.

## 1. Core Workflow: The Memecoin Lifecycle

Trading memecoins requires a rapid, iterative process that balances speed with safety. Follow this sequence when evaluating a potential trade:

1. **Discovery**: Identify trending tokens via social media (X, Telegram), DexScreener trending, or "new pairs" bots.
2. **Safety Audit**: Perform an immediate contract check to identify red flags like mint functions, honeypots, or unlocked liquidity.
3. **Holder Analysis**: Use visualization tools to ensure the supply is not concentrated in a few developer-controlled "cluster" wallets.
4. **Strategic Execution**: Choose an entry strategy (Snipe, Momentum, or Mean Reversion) and set clear take-profit and stop-loss levels.
5. **Post-Trade Monitoring**: Track the social sentiment and volume to determine the optimal exit point before the "hype cycle" ends.

## 2. Risk Management Framework

Memecoin trading is inherently speculative. To survive the volatility, strictly adhere to these principles:

- **Initial Recovery**: Sell half of your position once it reaches a 2x return. This secures your initial capital and lets the remaining "moonbag" run risk-free.
- **Strict Sizing**: Never allocate more than 1-2% of your total portfolio to a single micro-cap memecoin.
- **Mental Stop-Loss**: If a project's core narrative fails or a major red flag emerges post-entry, exit immediately regardless of the current price.
