# Memecoin Safety and Risk Management

## 1. Red Flags (How to Avoid Rug Pulls)
- **Mint Function**: Check if the developer can mint new tokens to dump on the market.
- **Honey Pot**: Attempt a small "test sell" or use tools like RugCheck.xyz or Token Sniffer.
- **Locked Liquidity**: Verify that the liquidity pool is locked (LP tokens burned or in a locker like PinkLock).
- **Holder Concentration**: Avoid tokens where the top 10 holders (excluding exchanges/burn) own > 20% of the supply.
- **Contract Verification**: Ensure the contract is verified on Etherscan/Solscan.

## 2. Risk Management (The 1% Rule)
- **Position Sizing**: Never put more than 1-2% of your total portfolio into a single memecoin.
- **Take Profit (TP)**: Sell 50% of the position when it doubles (2x) to "get the initial out."
- **Stop Loss (SL)**: Set mental or hard stop losses at 30-50% below entry.
- **Portfolio Allocation**: Memecoins should occupy < 10% of your total crypto holdings.
