# Memecoin Trading Strategies

## 1. The Sniper Strategy (Early Entry)
Focuses on entering a position within seconds of liquidity being added.
- **Tools**: Photon, BullX, Trojan on Solana.
- **Criteria**: Contract verified, liquidity locked, no mint function, top holders < 10%.
- **Risk**: Extremely high (99% of new launches fail or rug).

## 2. The Narrative Follower (Momentum)
Trading based on viral trends, tweets from influencers (e.g., Elon Musk), or cultural events.
- **Tools**: X (Twitter), DexScreener (Trending), LunarCrush.
- **Execution**: Buy when social volume spikes but before price reaches "blow-off top".
- **Exit**: Sell into the hype, usually 24-48 hours after the peak of social mentions.

## 3. The Technical Bounce (Mean Reversion)
Trading established memecoins (e.g., PEPE, DOGE, WIF) during corrections.
- **Tools**: TradingView, Birdeye.
- **Indicators**: RSI oversold on 4H/1D, support at previous ATH, Fibonacci retracement (0.618 level).
- **Exit**: Target previous resistance levels or 20-30% profit.

## 4. Wallet Tracking (Copy-Trading)
Identifying "Smart Money" wallets and mirroring their trades.
- **Tools**: Solscan, DexCheck, Bubblemaps.
- **Method**: Find wallets that consistently buy low and sell high on successful projects.
- **Risk**: Delay in tracking can lead to being "exit liquidity" for the smart money.
