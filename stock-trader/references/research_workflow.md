# Stock Research and Prediction Workflow

## 1. Discovery and Screening

Identify potential undervalued stocks using these screening criteria:
- **Low P/E and P/B**: Focus on the bottom 25% of the sector.
- **Positive Free Cash Flow**: Avoid "value traps" with negative cash flow.
- **Insider Buying**: Filter for companies with significant recent executive purchases.
- **Institutional Accumulation**: Look for stocks with increasing ownership by top funds.

## 2. Deep Dive Fundamental Analysis

Once a candidate is identified, perform a deep dive into its financial health:
- **Balance Sheet Audit**: Check the Debt-to-Equity ratio and Current Ratio (liquidity).
- **Income Statement Trends**: Verify 5-year revenue and net income growth consistency.
- **Cash Flow Analysis**: Ensure that net income is backed by real cash from operations.
- **Competitive Moat**: Research the company's market position, patents, and brand strength.

## 3. Historical Prediction and Price Targeting

Develop a fact-based prediction for the stock's future performance:
- **Mean Reversion Analysis**: Calculate the 5-year average P/E and apply it to current earnings to find a "fair value" target.
- **Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)**: Project future cash flows and discount them to the present to estimate intrinsic value.
- **Historical Support and Resistance**: Use long-term charts to identify where the stock has historically bottomed out and peaked.
- **Catalyst Identification**: List upcoming events (earnings reports, product launches, regulatory changes) that could trigger a price move.
